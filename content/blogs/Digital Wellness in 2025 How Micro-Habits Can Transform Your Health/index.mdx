## 9 Digital Wellness Micro-Habits for 2025

### 1. The **Notification Audit**
- Turn off non-essential alerts.  
- Keep only calls, texts, reminders.  
- Can reduce **screen pickups by 40%**.  

---

### 2. The **20-20-20 Rule**
Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.  
- Prevents digital eye strain.  
- Recommended by the [American Optometric Association](https://www.aoa.org/).  

---

### 3. The **Phone-Free Morning**
Delay phone use for **30 minutes after waking**.  
- Boosts clarity and mood.  
- Prevents cortisol spikes.  

---

### 4. The **Digital Sunset**
Stop screens **1 hour before bed**.  
- Blue light suppresses melatonin by up to 23%.  
- Improves deep sleep cycles.  

---

### 5. The **Mindful Scroll**
Before opening apps, ask:  
üëâ ‚ÄúAm I scrolling with purpose or out of habit?‚Äù  
- Reduces unconscious usage by 1‚Äì2 hours daily.  

---

### 6. The **Workspace Reset**
- Keep phone out of reach while working.  
- Store it in a drawer or separate room.  
- Boosts productivity by 40‚Äì50%.  

---

### 7. The **Digital Sabbath**
Take **24 hours per week off screens**.  
- Enhances creativity.  
- Strengthens offline relationships.  

---

### 8. The **Micro-Break Habit**
For every 60 minutes of work:  
- Stand up, stretch, or walk 2 minutes.  
- Lowers stress and prevents back/neck pain.  

---

### 9. The **Gratitude Check-In**
Replace 5 minutes of doomscrolling with journaling or meditation.  
- Improves mood.  
- Trains mindful tech use.
